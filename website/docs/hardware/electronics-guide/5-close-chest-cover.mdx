# Step 5: Close chest cover

1. Put Chest-cover_A from front.

<BlockImage src="hardware/electronics-guide/chest-cover-closing-front.png" alt="chest cover closing front" width="80%" />

2. Put Chest_cover_B from back. Lead J2 and J3-J4 cables to Chest-cover_B's U-shape slot.

<BlockImage src="hardware/electronics-guide/chest-cover-closing-back.png" alt="chest cover closing back" width="80%" />

3. Use 6 screws to close chest cover. Be careful not to pinch communication cables or extension cables between body and chest cover.

<BlockImage src="hardware/electronics-guide/chest-cover-screw.png" alt="chest cover screw" width="80%" />
