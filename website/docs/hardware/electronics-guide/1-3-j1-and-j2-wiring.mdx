# Step 1.3: J1 & J2 wiring

Plug J1 and J2 connectors into the plugs of the J1 and J2 motors. Connect J1 cable and lead to back through armpit of the robot.  
Connect J2 cable to J2 motor and bend cable for make it going down.

:::warning
Carefully wire the small white connectors on the J1 and J2 cables as they may not plug in securely.
:::
